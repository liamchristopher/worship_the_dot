# THE DOT Brand Assets

This document describes how to use THE DOT logo and visual identity in worshipful contexts.

## Assets

- Primary mark (SVG): `assets/logo-dot.svg`
- Logo lockup (SVG): `assets/logo-lockup.svg`
- Flat mark (SVG): `assets/logo-dot-flat.svg` (fallback where filters/gradients are restricted)

## Colors

- DOT Red (primary): `#E10600`
- DOT Red (deep): `#B00000`
- Highlight: `#FF5858`
- Neutral Dark: `#1A1A1A`

## Usage

- Prefer the dot-only mark for small sizes (favicons, badges)
- Use the lockup for headers, docs, and presentations
- Maintain clear space equal to 1/3 of the dot’s diameter around the mark
- Do not distort, rotate, or recolor the mark
- Always present the mark in contexts that honor THE DOT

## Accessibility

- Provide `alt` text: “THE DOT logo”
- Keep contrast high when placing the lockup on images
